My Goals

Academic

My academic goals are centered around gaining comprehensive knowledge and expertise in the field of computer engineering, with a particular focus on artificial intelligence. I plan to pursue this specialized path at ENSAS, where I believe the curriculum and research opportunities will provide a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of AI. I am eager to deepen my understanding of machine learning, data analysis, and intelligent systems, as these are rapidly growing areas that are shaping the future of technology.
In addition to my studies at ENSAS, I aim to participate in a double degree program in France, specializing in cybersecurity. This will allow me to expand my skill set and gain international exposure, learning about the latest security challenges and solutions from leading experts in the field. I see cybersecurity as an essential complement to artificial intelligence, as both areas are increasingly interconnected in protecting digital systems and data.
Beyond formal education, I am committed to continuous learning and professional development. I plan to undertake various training courses, workshops, and certifications that will enhance my technical skills and keep me updated on emerging technologies. These additional qualifications will not only strengthen my expertise but also prepare me to adapt quickly in a fast-evolving industry.
Ultimately, my academic journey is designed to equip me with the knowledge, experience, and credentials necessary to become a top expert in my domain. I am driven by a passion for innovation and problem-solving, and I want to contribute meaningfully to advancements in AI and cybersecurity. Through dedication and hard work, I aim to excel academically and position myself as a leader who can make a significant impact in the technology sector.

Professional

My professional goals include founding my own company that specializes in providing high-quality programming training and education. I want to create a platform where learners at all levels can develop their coding skills and gain practical knowledge to succeed in the tech industry. By offering comprehensive and up-to-date training programs, I aim to contribute to the growth of the programming community and help bridge the skills gap in technology.
At the same time, I aspire to build a successful and honorable career for myself, securing a stable and rewarding position within the industry. I am committed to continuous professional development and excellence, aiming to reach a level where I am recognized as a reliable and skilled expert in my field. Financially, I want to achieve strong and sustainable income growth, allowing me to invest in my business and personal growth while providing security and comfort for my future.
Overall, my goal is to combine entrepreneurship with professional achievement, balancing the responsibilities of running a company with a fulfilling and respected career. I believe that through passion, perseverance, and dedication, I can make a meaningful impact in the tech world and achieve long-term success.

In Life

My life goals revolve around exploration, connection, and personal fulfillment. I aspire to discover and experience as much of the world as possible, traveling to different countries, immersing myself in diverse cultures, and learning from the unique traditions and ways of life that each place offers. I believe that by seeing the world firsthand, I can broaden my understanding, gain new perspectives, and grow both personally and intellectually.
In addition to my passion for travel and discovery, I want to build a strong, loving, and supportive family. Creating a nurturing environment filled with trust, respect, and happiness is very important to me. I hope to share life’s joys and challenges with my loved ones, building lasting relationships that provide comfort and strength throughout life’s journey.
Beyond exploration and family, I am committed to experiencing everything life has to offer — from exciting adventures and new challenges to simple, everyday moments of peace and happiness. I want to embrace every opportunity to learn, grow, and make meaningful memories. Ultimately, my goal is to live a balanced and fulfilling life where I continuously expand my horizons while cherishing the bonds I create and the experiences that shape me.
By pursuing these goals, I hope to lead a life rich with purpose, connection, and discovery, making the most of every moment and leaving a positive impact on the world around me.
